Privacy Policy for Guess It!

Effective date: April 19, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Aamir Shakir ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the mobile application Guess It! ("the App").

Summary

We do not collect any personal information from users of Guess It! The App is a standalone number-guessing game that runs entirely on your device.

Information we collect

We do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, identifiers, analytics, usage statistics, advertising identifiers, or device information from users of the App.

Information stored on your device

Guess It! stores your best scores and selected difficulty preference locally on your device using standard browser storage (localStorage). This information never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or to any third party. You can clear this information at any time by using the "Reset best scores" option inside the App or by uninstalling the App.

Third parties

The App does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, crash-reporting, or tracking services. No data is transmitted from the App to any external server operated by us or by a third party.

Children's privacy

Guess It! is intended for general audiences aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. Because the App collects no personal data from any user, it does not collect personal data from children either.

Permissions

Guess It! does not request access to your location, contacts, camera, microphone, photos, or any other sensitive device permissions.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page along with an updated effective date. Continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated Policy.
